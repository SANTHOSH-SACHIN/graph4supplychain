# Graph Convolutional GRU (GConvGRU)

**Dataset**: Chickenpox

GConvGRU combines graph convolutional layers with gated recurrent units (GRU) to enhance the temporal modeling of dynamic graphs.

## Results on Chickenpox Dataset:

- **MSE**: 0.9676
- **MAE**: 0.6274
- **MAPE**: 583.1867%
- **RMSE**: 0.9837
- **R-squared**: 0.0390
